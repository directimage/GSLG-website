Education

Here at GS Lighting Group, we have built an organization on the importance of education in the ever changing Lighting Industry. The need for energy efficient lighting systems, innovative luminaire design and technologies that control these systems, is driven by the demands of each design project for the built environment. Lighting and its related technologies are on a continued path of evolution, and to keep up with this path, our current LED lighting Industry needs an even higher level of Education for Professionals and owners alike. We facilitates this Education process to connect our Specifying Clients, Contractors and customer base with our leading Manufacturers to provide the valuable process of understanding of new technologies and designs.

Lighting Design

Our GS Lighting Group lighting design team works within industry standards in technological innovation and product development to support each client group with sourcing leading edge LED products and control systems to meet their wants, needs and expectations. No request is too small for our Professional Lighting Design Specialists; whether you are looking for basic luminaire recommendations or have a large project that needs lighting study documents and photometric support, our AGi32 Specialists and Lighting Control Designers are all-hands-on-deck to meet your deadlines. Our goal is to ultimately support the design process to create environments that are healthy, provide efficiency in the workspace, and reduce carbon footprints with sustainable lighting systems while achieving building innovation.

Design Collaborations

As an extension to our Lighting Design services, our design team works with Specifiers to set future lighting trends for the communities in which we reside. Our mission is to facilitate and build strong partnerships between our design community, manufacturing partners, other involved professionals and the end-users that ultimately occupy and reside in the designed spaces. Our team of professionals provide technical lighting knowledge to collaborate with the design community of Engineers, Architects and Designers in the process of selecting effective lighting solutions for the construction of any building type. As an extension of our Energy/Retrofit Division, we also collaborate with our Distribution and Contracting partners in design-build and retrofit projects.

Project Site Review

Today's process in lighting design is an equal partnership of all disciplines; including owners, Architects, Interior Designers, Engineers, Contractors, Distributors, Agency networks and Manufacturers. As a team, we work to link all partners together to achieve design objectives and meet various design intent.

Providing effective design solutions brings a responsibility to energy goals, long term cost avoidance and budgetary price structures that provide accurate construction cost to plan a project objectives with. Our ultimate goal in the project site review process is to work alongside the professionals and end-users to bring transparency to the design process.

Project Management

Our Project Managers and Customer Service team are vital to the success of every project. They are a part of your lighting design team from conception to delivery and after sale services. They strive to keep a clear communication channel to provide you with project expectations in a timely and clear fashion.

Energy / Lighting Audit - Retrofit

Our Energy Division team has built a reputation for providing customers with a strategic roadmap to achieving their goals of Energy efficiency for any existing and new spaces. Our ultimate goal is to provide a retrofit solution that meets and exceeds the clients goals and expectations.

Our Auditors follow a professional and thorough process and procedures manual. This involves training of involved parties, reviewing current lighting system problems, reading existing light levels in the environment, understanding all client objectives in the retrofit plan, and designing a new LED lighting solution. We then generate a final proposal with an energy efficient execution plan to help you understand your Return on Investment (ROI).
