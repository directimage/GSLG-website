Who We Are


GS Lighting Group is a leading lighting service company representing lighting manufacturers to the design, engineering, distribution and contracting communities.

Our focus is in Architectural Lighting Systems for the commercial, education, healthcare, athletic, industrial and residential markets.

History


GS Lighting Group was established in 1990 with a focus on improving any space with the use of indirect lighting. Today, we are a leading manufacturer’s representative in the lighting industry. We’re continually linking lighting applications with quality lighting from leading manufactures, with design and architectural intent.

Mission Statement


GS Lighting Group is committed to providing our customers with unparalleled support and the highest possible level of service in our pursuit of business excellence. Our goal is to help inspire creativity by offering the highest quality lighting products that meet the requirements of virtually any project.

"Everyone has the right to quality light"

Vision


GS Lighting Group is a lighting agency that envisions a brighter and more energy efficient future.

The last two decades have seen many changes in the lighting industry, but our vision to improve an environment with light still remains our focus.
