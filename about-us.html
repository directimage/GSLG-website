ABOUT US

With over 30 years of experience in the industry, our independence combined with our entrepreneurial mindset has been critical to our success with customers, clients and manufacturing partners.

  • Our Clients are professional Engineers, Architects, Interior Designers and Professional Lighting Designers.
  • Our Customers include Electrical Contractors, Electrical Distributors, Municipalities and National Accounts.
  • Our Staff are highly trained individuals with experience in various backgrounds including illuminating engineering, interior design, distribution, and construction management.
  • Our lighting design centre is located in the heart of Ontario to be able to better service out clients, customers and partners.

Our fully resourced office and Inspiration Center is located in Cambridge Ontario, with over 15 highly trained and dedicated staff members. Our training center incorporates the latest in applied lighting techniques, technologies and innovative products.

We also demonstrate the latest technologies in lighting controls showcased throughout our facility and within our interactive, dedicated lighting training lab.

OUR HISTORY

GS Lighting Group was established in 1990 with a focus on improving any space with the use of indirect lighting. Today, we are a leading manufacturer’s representative in the lighting industry. We’re continually linking lighting applications with quality lighting from leading manufactures, with design and architectural intent.

OUR MISSION

GS Lighting Group provides quality service, creative solutions, and opportunities for growth for our manufacturers, customers, employees, and the community in which we reside. We are committed to excellence, which we strive to achieve by focusing on honesty, integrity, education, and attention to detail.

OUR VALUES

  • We bring integrity to our approach in business practices.
  • We are all about sustainability. Every day our products are getting better and lasting longer, and so we have made that same commitment to the Earth, by incorporating recycling programs, using natural light, and of course using the energy efficient lamps that we sell.
  • We love to listen about your projects. Our full engagement will help you make the best of them.
  • Watch out when we start talking about lighting, because we love our job with a passion.
